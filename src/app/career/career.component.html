Security Service Careers

At Surefire, we're dedicated to expanding our team of individuals who share our commitment to excellence and safeguarding what matters most. Whether you're seeking full-time or part-time work, day or night shifts, we have opportunities to suit your preferences. We take pride in our exceptional employee retention rates, which are a testament to the clear pathways for advancement within Surefire. Our cohesive team shares a unified mindset and consistently delivers top-notch security services.

Recognizing the uniqueness of each employee, we strive to match individual strengths, talents, and schedules with suitable assignments, ensuring not only employee satisfaction but also client contentment. Join us in making a meaningful impact on our communities while advancing your career at Surefire.

Qualifications

Our qualifications for employment include the following, at minimum:

  • must be a US citizen or an alien lawfully authorized to work in the United States
  • must have an honorable discharge from military (if served)
  • must pass a background check and drug screen
  • must be at least 18 years of age (21 for some positions)
  • must be willing to pass medical and/or psychological tests as a condition of employment or continued employment (including tests for substance abuse)
  • must meet minimum requirements of state licensing for security guard employment
